# insights.cloudgeeks.com.au Brand Guidelines

## Brand Identity

**Tagline**: Practical Cloud & IT Solutions for Australian Business
**Position**: Trusted technology advisor for Australian SMBs
**Author**: Ash Ganda

## Target Audience

### Primary
- **IT managers** at Australian SMBs (20-200 employees)
- **SMB owners** making technology decisions
- **Operations managers** implementing digital solutions

### Secondary
- Startup technical founders
- Non-profit technology leads
- Government department IT teams

### Audience Characteristics
- Budget-conscious, need clear ROI justification
- May not have dedicated IT teams
- Value practical, implementable advice
- Want Australian-specific context (compliance, vendors, support)
- Prefer proven solutions over bleeding edge

## Voice & Tone

### Core Tone
- **Trusted advisor**: Like having a knowledgeable friend in IT
- **Practical**: Focus on what actually works
- **Australian**: Local context, local examples, local vendors
- **Accessible**: Technical concepts explained clearly
- **Honest**: Acknowledge limitations and costs

### Writing Style
- Use plain language, explain technical terms
- Include Australian dollar estimates where relevant
- Reference Australian compliance (Privacy Act, Essential Eight)
- Provide step-by-step guidance
- Acknowledge SMB constraints (budget, time, expertise)

### Vocabulary
**Use**: practical, Australian businesses, cost-effective, implement, secure, grow, streamline, local, support, proven
**Avoid**: enterprise, complex, cutting-edge, revolutionary, massive, expensive

## Content Structure

### Ideal Article Structure
1. **Problem Hook** (100-150 words): Common SMB challenge
2. **Context** (150-200 words): Why this matters for Australian businesses
3. **Solution Overview** (200-300 words): High-level approach
4. **Implementation Guide** (300-400 words): Practical steps
5. **Australian Context** (200-300 words): Local considerations, vendors, compliance
6. **Cost/Benefit Analysis** (150-200 words): ROI justification
7. **Next Steps** (100-150 words): How to get started

### Word Count Target
- **Minimum**: 1,200 words
- **Target**: 1,500 words
- **Maximum**: 1,800 words

## Australian Business Context

### Compliance Considerations
- Privacy Act 1988
- Essential Eight (ASD)
- Industry-specific regulations (APRA, health, etc.)
- Data sovereignty requirements

### Local Vendor Ecosystem
- Australian cloud providers (Vault Cloud, AUCloud)
- Local MSPs and support options
- Australian data center locations

### Business Culture
- Acknowledgment of Australian business challenges
- Seasonal considerations (financial year, holiday trading)
- Regional differences across states

## Brand Don'ts

- Never ignore Australian-specific context
- Never assume enterprise-level budgets or resources
- Never use excessive technical jargon without explanation
- Never recommend solutions without cost considerations
- Never overlook security and compliance requirements
- Never present only one vendor option without alternatives
