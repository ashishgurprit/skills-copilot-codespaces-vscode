## Educational Content Best Practices

### The Value Test
```
Before posting, ask:
1. Would someone screenshot this?
2. Would someone save this?
3. Would someone share this with a colleague?
4. Does this solve a real problem?

If not, revise until yes.
```

### Making Complex Simple
```
Expert trap: Assuming knowledge
Solution: Write for someone 2 years behind you

‚ùå "Leverage synergistic paradigm shifts"
‚úÖ "Work with others to find new approaches"

‚ùå "Implement a robust CI/CD pipeline"
‚úÖ "Automatically test and deploy your code changes"
```

### The Specific Beats Generic Rule
```
‚ùå "Use good tools"
‚úÖ "Use Notion for docs, Slack for chat, Loom for updates"

‚ùå "Post at good times"
‚úÖ "Post Tuesday-Thursday, 8-10 AM in your audience's timezone"

‚ùå "Write better content"
‚úÖ "Hook in first 2 lines, one idea per line, end with question"
```

### Format for Scanning

People don't read, they scan.

Make your posts scannable:
‚Ä¢ Bold key words/phrases
‚Ä¢ Use symbols (‚Ü≥ ‚Üí ‚Ä¢ ‚úÖ ‚ùå)
‚Ä¢ Numbers for lists
‚Ä¢ Short lines
‚Ä¢ White space
‚Ä¢ Clear hierarchy

### Credibility Without Bragging
